Stretch smarter.
Zero redundancy.

FlexFlow is muscle-first. Select what you are training, choose your depth, get exactly the stretches you need — deduplicated, ordered by coverage, and explained.

Every plan is editable. Replace any stretch with a targeted alternative. Add from the full library. Remove what doesn't fit. Save named plans for your regular training splits.

Quick mode surfaces the highest-coverage stretches. Detailed mode covers every anatomical sub-region.

⚑ The FlexFlow rule
Never static stretch before training. Activation and dynamic first, static after. FlexFlow enforces this by design.
